Game of Thrones vs. Lord of the Rings

Game of Thrones is the first book in the series A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin (I unfortunately only found the .txt file for this one). It is being compared to the Lord of the Rings trilogy by J. R. R. Tolkien.

3-Gram Comparison

Game of Thrones has 292,727 total words while the entire Lord of the Rings trilogy combined by has about double that at 576,459 words. Despie LOTR having nearly double the word count, both 3-grams have an comparable frequency at first, and then GOT 3-Gram frequency actually begins to out number the LOTR frequency.

Another note is that the top GOT 3-grams seem to be more specific to the series than the LOTR grams. For example, GOT has grams like:

  • the king s
  • king s landing
  • the night s
  • night s watch
  • the old bear

Whereas the only series specific 3-gram for LOTR in the top 15 is:

  • in the shire
